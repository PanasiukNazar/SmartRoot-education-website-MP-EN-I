Privacy Policy

Welcome to the privacy policy page of our website dedicated to educational services. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we handle your personal data.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") applies to all users of our website and governs the collection and use of personal information that we collect as you use our services. By using our website, you agree to the terms of this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Includes your name, email address, phone number, and other information you provide when registering or subscribing to our newsletter.
  • Usage Information: We may collect data about how you use our website, including IP address, browser type, pages you visit, and time of access.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to collect information about your preferences and to improve your experience on our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

The collected information may be used for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving our services;
  • Personalizing the user experience;
  • Communicating with you regarding your inquiries or questions;
  • Sending informational newsletters and updates;
  • Analyzing the use of our website and identifying trends.

4. Disclosure of Information

We will not disclose your personal information to third parties except in the following cases:

  • It is required by law or legal procedures;
  • It is necessary to protect the rights and property of our website;
  • It is related to the transfer of business, where we may transfer your information along with other assets.

5. Information Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. You are responsible for protecting your account and password.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information;
  • Request correction or deletion of your data;
  • Opt-out of receiving marketing materials at any time;
  • Challenge the processing of your personal data.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your behavior on our website. You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, but this may affect the functionality of the website.

8. Links to Other Sites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these sites. We recommend that you review the privacy policy of each site you visit.

9. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Policy at any time. All changes will be published on this page, and you are responsible for reviewing the policy regularly. Your continued use of the website after the changes will be considered as your agreement to the new Policy.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions or comments regarding this Policy, please contact us. We will strive to respond to your inquiries as quickly as possible.

11. Applicable Law

This Policy is governed by the applicable laws and is interpreted in accordance with them. Any disputes arising in connection with this Policy will be subject to judicial review.

12. Data Usage Terms

We may use your information to analyze trends and improve the quality of our services. We may also use aggregated data for marketing and research purposes.

13. Consent to Data Processing

By using our website, you consent to the processing of your personal information in accordance with this Policy. If you do not agree with the terms, please do not use our website.

14. Data Access and Correction

You have the right to request access to your data and correct any inaccuracies. To do so, please contact us with the appropriate request.

15. Data Retention

We will retain your information only for the time necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or for the period required by law.

16. Age Restrictions

Our website is intended for individuals over the age of 18. We do not intentionally collect personal data from children. If we learn that we have collected such data, we will take steps to remove it.

17. Termination of Data Usage

You may at any time stop using our services and request the deletion of your information. We will comply with your request in accordance with applicable law.

18. Anonymity and Confidentiality

We place a high value on user anonymity. We do not collect personal data without your consent and use it only for the purposes described in this Policy.

19. Data Security Policy

We take all reasonable measures to protect your data. However, you must also ensure the security of your information by not disclosing your password to third parties.

20. Notes and Comments

We may retain your comments and feedback to improve our service. However, we will not disclose your personal information for these purposes.

21. Use of Information for Research

We may use anonymous data for research and analysis, which will help us improve the quality of the services we provide.

22. Disclaimer

While we strive to ensure the security and confidentiality of your data, we cannot guarantee complete protection from unauthorized access and other threats. You use our website at your own risk.

23. Conclusion

Thank you for choosing our website for your educational needs. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. If you have any questions regarding this Policy, please feel free to contact us.
